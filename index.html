Dongryul M. Kim
[ M. is only for publications. | 🔈 Dong-Nyool Geem ]
Hi! I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Dept. of Mathematics at Yale University.
My advisor is Prof. Hee Oh.

* I am on the job market in the fall of 2025.

I received my B.S. degree in Mathematical Sciences from KAIST on Feb. 2021, where I was a member of the Geometric Topology research group under Prof. Harry Hyungryul Baik.

My research lies in Geometry, Topology, and Dynamics with the following keywords:

- Discrete subgroups of Lie groups and Homogeneous dynamics
- Kleinian groups and (relatively) Anosov groups
- Deformations and Rigidity theorems
- Mapping class groups and dynamics on Teichmüller spaces and Curve complexes
- Geometric group theory
- Random walks on groups and their stationary measures
