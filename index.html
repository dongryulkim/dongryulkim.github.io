About Me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the Dept. of Mathematics at Yale University since Sep. 2021. I have been supported by the KFAS Ph.D. Study-Abroad Scholarship.
Previously, I was a researcher of Team for Academic Research on GEometric Topology at KAIST, under the supervision of Prof. Harry Hyungryul Baik. I received my B.S. in Mathematical Sciences from KAIST on Feb. 2021.

Research Interests

My mathematical interests lie in Geometric topology, Low-dimensional topology, and Dynamical systems. It includes mapping class groups, hyperbolic geometry, theory of 3-manifolds, and Teichmüller theory.
